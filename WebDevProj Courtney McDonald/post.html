Picture yourself here, without a worry in the world

That's right, let's make this dream a reality

Posted by Courtney McDonald on June 21, 2023

Did you know that Americans have let over 765 million vacation days go unused? Think of all the time you could've spent exploring, destressing, and bonding with loved ones.

It's take we take charge of our time and our dreams. That bucket list on your desk is nothing but a peace of paper getting dusty if you don't start making plans to make dreams a reality.

What's the first step you say? Pick a time. Mark it out on your calendar now, whether it's in 2 weeks, 2 months, or 2 years, just do it. Next, figure out if anyone's coming with you. If so, collaborate together on your travel bucket list to decide on your destination.

Great! Where'd you pick? Germany? The Galapagos islands? Hong Kong Disney? Now's the time to start booking flights and hotels, doing your research can save you a dime and stress in the future. Book early!

The Fun Part

You're getting closer. Now for my favorite part. Itinerary! Research the top places to go and see and pick your priorities. Next, plan what days you'll do them, and research fun restaurants or stops on the way to each of your destinations.

Well done! You've figured out the trip, so don't back out now! Money comes back but experiences are once in a lifetime! So enjoy this experience and relax! You won't regret it.

Packing

The trip is approaching, time to pack! Packing can be stressful, you wanna pack light make sure you have everything you'll need. Start by making a list and checking it off as you go. Don't stress if you forget the toothpaste, there'll always be a convenience store along the way you can stop at!

To go places and do things that have never been done before – that’s what living is all about.
